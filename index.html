About Hotel De Zews

Hotel De Zews is located at Osu, a prime suburb in Accra. The hotel prides itself as one of the most affordable in Osu, offering a myriad of services. The Osu Suburb is also known for it's all-day-round vibrancy, including night life. The suburb also happens to have one of the busiest streets in Accra - Oxford street - where almost any artefact can be purchased. The hotel happens to be just a distance from some of Ghana's best shopping malls & finest restaurants offering local & continental dishes. We are also just 3 minutes drive to the Beach, 15 minutes to Kotoka International Airport & 10 minutes to Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park & Other heritage sites. As a result, a stay at Hotel De Zews provides guest with the needed convenience.
